## 1. Introduction

At Amotive, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it — in plain language.

We operate in the European Union and comply with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

## 2. What We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you fill out our intake form or contact us, we may collect:

- Your name and email address
- Business name and industry
- Website URL
- Service interest and budget
- Business goals and project details

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may collect:

- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring source
- Browser type and device information
- IP address (anonymized where possible)

## 3. How We Collect It

- **Google Forms** — Our intake and inquiry forms are powered by Google Forms.
- **Email** — Direct communications via hello@amotive.io.
- **Google Analytics** — Website analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site.

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

- **Deliver our services** — understand your needs, scope projects, and execute work.
- **Communicate with you** — respond to inquiries, send proposals, share updates.
- **Improve our services** — analyze website usage to make the experience better.

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Ever.

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

| Basis | When it applies |
|---|---|
| **Consent** | When you submit a form or opt in to communications. |
| **Contractual necessity** | When processing is required to deliver services you've engaged us for. |
| **Legitimate interest** | Website analytics and improving our services. |

## 6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

- **Google Forms** — Form submissions and data collection. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Analytics** — Website traffic analysis. [Google Analytics Terms](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/terms/us/)
- **GitHub Pages** — Website hosting. [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)

Each provider has their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Client data** is retained for the duration of our business relationship and up to **2 years** after the last engagement, for reference and continuity.
- **Inquiry data** (form submissions from non-clients) is retained for up to **1 year**.
- **Analytics data** is retained according to Google Analytics' default settings (currently 14 months).

You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 9).

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

- Secure access controls on all accounts and tools.
- Encrypted communications (HTTPS, TLS).
- Limited access — only team members who need your data to perform their work can access it.

No system is 100% secure, but we take this seriously and continuously review our practices.

## 9. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU (or where applicable law provides similar rights), you have the right to:

- **Access** — Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Rectification** — Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure** — Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Restriction** — Ask us to limit how we use your data.
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Object** — Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Withdraw consent** — At any time, without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **hello@amotive.io**. We will respond within **30 days**.

## 10. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics purposes (via Google Analytics). These are not used for advertising or tracking across other sites. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16 years of age.
