# Casa Verde Restaurant — Digital Transformation Case Study

*Client: Casa Verde Portuguese Restaurant, Lisbon | Project Duration: 4 weeks | Investment: €2,500*

---

## The Challenge

Casa Verde had been serving authentic Portuguese cuisine in Lisbon's Príncipe Real district for 8 years, but their digital presence was stuck in 2015. Their outdated website looked more like a placeholder than a restaurant that deserved the Michelin Guide mention it had earned.

**The problems were clear:**
- Website built in 2016, not mobile-responsive, no online reservation system
- Competitors with sleek sites and seamless booking were stealing customers
- Google Business Profile was unclaimed and unoptimized  
- Zero social media presence beyond a dormant Facebook page
- Relying entirely on walk-ins and word-of-mouth in an increasingly digital market
- Monthly revenue declining 15% year-over-year as foot traffic decreased post-pandemic

**The wake-up call:** Casa Verde's owner, Miguel, discovered customers were bouncing from their website in under 10 seconds and booking at competitors instead. "We were losing customers before they even tasted our food," he told us.

## Our Solution

We implemented our **Website Redesign Package** (€2,500) with a focus on conversion and local discovery:

### Week 1-2: Foundation & Design
- **Mobile-first website redesign** featuring high-quality food photography and Portuguese cultural elements
- **Integrated reservation system** using OpenTable API for seamless booking
- **Local SEO optimization** targeting "Portuguese restaurant Lisbon" and "authentic bacalhau Príncipe Real"
- **Google Business Profile takeover** with fresh photos, updated hours, and menu integration

### Week 3: Content & Technical
- **Menu digitization** with English translations and dietary indicators
- **Speed optimization** reducing load time from 8.2s to 1.3s
- **Schema markup** for restaurant details, reviews, and opening hours
- **Contact form integration** with automated confirmation emails

### Week 4: Launch & Optimization
- **Google Ads setup** targeting high-intent local searches ("Portuguese restaurant near me")
- **Social media profiles** creation and optimization (Instagram, Facebook)
- **Review generation system** with automated follow-up emails to diners
- **Analytics tracking** for reservation conversions and user behavior

### Local Visibility

- **Google Business Profile views increased 485%** with 89% positive review sentiment

### Revenue Impact
- **€15,000 additional monthly revenue** within 8 weeks of launch
- **18% increase in average table size** due to improved menu presentation

### User Experience Metrics
- **Website bounce rate dropped from 78% to 23%**

## What Made It Work

**Local SEO mastery:** We identified that 67% of potential customers searched "Portuguese restaurant + [Lisbon neighborhood]" — optimizing for hyper-local terms drove qualified traffic.

**Photography investment:** We coordinated with a food photographer during the redesign. High-quality images of signature dishes increased online reservation rates by 156%.

**Reservation funnel optimization:** The new system reduced booking friction from 6 clicks to 2, with automatic confirmation and reminder emails that decreased no-shows by 31%.

**Mobile-first approach:** With 73% of restaurant searches happening on mobile, the responsive design was crucial for conversion.
