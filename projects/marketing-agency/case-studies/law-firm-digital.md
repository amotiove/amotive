**The situation in September 2024:**

"We realized our future clients were researching us online and finding nothing," explains João. "Meanwhile, less experienced firms with better websites were winning business we should have had."

## SEO Strategy Deep Dive

**Keyword Research & Content Mapping:**
We identified that potential clients searched for solutions, not lawyers. Instead of targeting "melhor advogado Lisboa," we focused on:
- "Como registar empresa Portugal" (How to register company Portugal)
- "Contrato trabalho startup" (Startup employment contract)
- "Direitos trabalhador Portugal" (Employee rights Portugal)

**Content Marketing Approach:**
- **Educational articles:** 65% of content focused on explaining Portuguese business law in plain language
- **Case studies:** 20% featured anonymized successful client outcomes  
- **Legal updates:** 15% covered recent law changes affecting businesses
