# Momentum Fitness — From Empty Classes to Waitlists

*Client: Momentum Fitness Boutique Gym, Porto | Campaign Duration: 6 months | Investment: €3,500/month*

---

## The Challenge

Momentum Fitness had the space, the equipment, and passionate trainers — but empty classes. Located in Porto's trendy Cedofeita district, this 200m² boutique gym was struggling to fill its high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and yoga classes.

**The situation in January 2025:**
- **78 active members** (capacity: 450)
- **Average class size: 4 people** (optimal: 12-15)
- **Zero social media presence** beyond a basic Instagram with 247 followers
- **Marketing budget: €0** — relying purely on word-of-mouth
- **Revenue: €7,200/month** from membership fees alone
- **Owner stress level: Maximum** — considering closing within 6 months

"I was teaching HIIT to two people in a space designed for twenty," recalls owner Catarina. "Something had to change, fast."

## Our Strategy

We deployed our **Growth Package** (€3,500/month) focusing on community building and performance marketing:

### Month 1: Foundation (January)
**Website & Social Setup**
- Mobile-optimized website with class booking system
- Instagram content strategy: workout tips + behind-the-scenes
- Google Business Profile optimization
- **Ad Spend:** €1,200 | **New Members:** 12

**Initial Results:** Instagram grew to 892 followers, first online bookings started

### Month 2: Content Amplification (February)  
**Social Media & Influencer Outreach**
- Daily Instagram Reels featuring trainers and member transformations

- Google Ads launch targeting "HIIT Porto" and "boutique gym Cedofeita"
- **Ad Spend:** €1,800 | **New Members:** 28

**Breakthrough:** First class waitlist for Saturday morning HIIT

### Month 3: Momentum Building (March)
**Community & Performance Focus**

**Key Milestone:** Instagram reached 2,847 followers, 85% class capacity

### Month 4: Scale & Systemize (April)
**Advanced Targeting & Retargeting**
- Lookalike audiences based on existing member data

**Final State:** Waitlists for prime time classes, expansion planning underway

## Campaign Breakdown

**Top Performing Ads:**
1. **"Transform in 30 Days" video testimonial** — 6.8x ROAS, 2.3% CTR
2. **"Free Week Trial" Instagram carousel** — 4.9x ROAS, 178 conversions
3. **"HIIT Class Preview" YouTube pre-roll** — 3.7x ROAS, highest retention rate

**Audience Insights:**
- **Primary demographic:** Women 25-40, household income €35K+
- **Peak engagement:** Tuesday/Thursday 7-9 PM
- **Conversion path:** Instagram → Website → Trial booking → Membership

**Content Strategy:**
- **40% educational:** Workout tips, nutrition advice, form corrections
- **30% social proof:** Member transformations, testimonials, community
- **20% behind-the-scenes:** Trainer personalities, gym culture
- **10% promotional:** Special offers, new classes, events

## What Made It Work

**Community-first approach:** Instead of selling gym memberships, we built a fitness community. Members became advocates, sharing their progress and bringing friends.

**Data-driven optimization:** We tracked everything — which ad creatives performed best, which class times had highest show-up rates, which members referred the most friends.

**Local influencer strategy:** Three partnerships with Porto fitness enthusiasts (500-2K followers each) generated higher engagement than celebrity endorsements at 1/10th the cost.

**Retargeting mastery:** 67% of new members visited the website multiple times before booking. Our retargeting sequences nurtured this consideration phase.
