## Long Description (Website / Investor / Full Profile — 500 words)

### Here's what nobody in marketing will tell you:

80% of small businesses have a digital presence that actively costs them customers. Not "fails to attract" — actually *repels*. Outdated websites, amateur social media, money burned on ads that target the wrong people.

And here's the uncomfortable part: most marketing agencies are fine with that. They'll charge you $1,500/month to post pretty pictures on Instagram, send you a PDF report full of vanity metrics, and call it a day. You get "engagement." They get paid. Nobody gets new customers.

**We decided to build the opposite of that.**

Amotive is a digital growth agency. Growth — not "awareness," not "impressions," not "brand synergy." Growth means more customers walking through your door, more calls from people ready to buy, more revenue on your bottom line.

**What we actually do:**

**Websites that sell.** Not digital brochures — conversion machines. Every section, every word, every button is engineered using behavioral psychology and A/B testing to turn visitors into leads. Our sites load in under 2 seconds, rank on Google, and look like a brand that charges 10x what you do. Starting at $2,500.

**Paid advertising that pays for itself.** We manage Google Ads and Meta campaigns with one obsession: return on ad spend. We don't "boost posts" — we build targeted funnels that track every dollar from click to sale. Most clients see 3-5x ROAS within the first 60 days. From $2,000/month.

**Social media that converts.** We create content strategies that do more than collect likes. We build authority, create demand, and give your audience a reason to choose you over the seven other options they're also looking at.

**Why businesses choose us:**

We're not the cheapest agency in the Pacific Northwest. We're not trying to be. We're the agency that shows you exactly where your money went and exactly what it earned. Every campaign has clear KPIs, real-time reporting, and a direct line to your strategist — not an intern.

We work with a select number of clients at any time. Not because we're exclusive for the sake of it, but because results require focus. If we take you on, you get our full attention.

**The uncomfortable truth:**

Your competitors are already investing in this. The restaurant down the street that's always packed? They're running Google Ads. The gym that opened six months ago and already has a waitlist? Professional social media strategy. The law firm that keeps showing up when you search? SEO and content marketing.

You can keep doing what you're doing. Or you can do what they did.

**One conversation.** That's all it takes to see what's possible. No pressure, no hard sell — just a clear picture of where you're leaving money on the table and what it would take to pick it up.
