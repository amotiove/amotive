## Phase 1: Portfolio Building (Week 2-4) 📸

### Get 3 Case Studies (Free/Discounted)
Strategy: Offer 3 local Lisbon businesses free or heavily discounted work in exchange for:
- Full creative control
- Testimonial (written + video)
- Before/after metrics
- Permission to use as case study

**Target businesses:**
1. A restaurant/cafe (visual, easy to show results)
2. A fitness studio/gym (engagement-heavy niche)
3. A small e-commerce/retail brand (conversion metrics)

### For Each Case Study, Document:
- Before state (screenshots of old website/social)
- What we built/changed
- After state (new design, new content)
- Metrics (traffic, engagement, followers, leads)
- Client testimonial quote
- 3-4 portfolio images

### Deliverables per Case Study Client:
- Website redesign (or build from scratch)
- Instagram content for 1 month (12-15 posts)
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Basic Google Ads campaign (€200-500 test budget)

## Phase 3: First Paying Clients (Month 2-3) 💰

### Sales Process (Flip the Script)
1. **Inbound lead** → 30-min discovery call (see PERSONALITY.md playbook)
2. **Discovery call** → Custom proposal within 48 hours
3. **Proposal** → Follow up once, then silence (no chasing)
4. **Signed** → Onboarding: brand questionnaire, access to accounts, kickoff call
5. **Delivery** → Website in 2-3 weeks, marketing starts Month 1
6. **Reporting** → Monthly strategy call + analytics report
7. **Retention** → Quarterly business reviews, upsell opportunities

### Onboarding Checklist (New Client)
- [ ] Signed contract + first invoice paid
- [ ] Brand questionnaire completed
- [ ] Access: Google Analytics, Search Console, social media accounts
- [ ] Competitor analysis (3-5 competitors)
- [ ] Content strategy document
- [ ] First month content calendar approved
- [ ] Ad accounts set up (if applicable)
