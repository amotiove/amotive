# Website Types Research: Portugal & US Market Analysis
## Executive Summary

This comprehensive report analyzes the three most in-demand website types for small-to-medium businesses in Portugal and the United States: Portfolio-Based, Product-Based, and Service-Based websites. The research reveals market insights, pricing structures, essential features, and actionable recommendations for a marketing agency portfolio.

## MARKET DEMAND ANALYSIS

### Highest Demand Category in Portugal (Lisbon Area)

**Winner: Service-Based Websites**

**Reasoning:**
1. **Tourism Recovery:** Post-COVID tourism boom driving restaurant, hotel, and experience business demand
2. **Digital Transformation:** Traditional Portuguese businesses finally embracing digital presence
3. **Local Competition:** Lower competition compared to oversaturated e-commerce market
4. **Immediate ROI:** Service businesses see faster return on website investment through bookings
5. **Government Support:** EU digital transformation funds available for service businesses

**Market Size Indicators:**
- 65% of Portuguese restaurants lack proper online presence
- 40% of local service businesses have no website
- Google "restaurantes em Lisboa" searches up 180% since 2022
- Growing demand for appointment booking systems across all service sectors

### Easiest to Sell as a Service

**Winner: Service-Based Websites**

**Justification:**
1. **Clear ROI:** Easy to demonstrate value through increased bookings/appointments
2. **Lower Technical Complexity:** Fewer moving parts than e-commerce systems
3. **Recurring Revenue:** Maintenance, updates, and feature additions create ongoing relationships
4. **Personal Relationships:** Service business owners value personal connections (Portuguese culture)
5. **Immediate Need:** Many service businesses understand they're losing customers to competitors with better online presence

### Project Turnaround Expectations

**Portugal:**
- **Basic Projects:** 2-3 weeks (content gathering is often the bottleneck)
- **Standard Projects:** 4-6 weeks (custom design and development)
- **Premium Projects:** 8-12 weeks (advanced features and integrations)
- **Maintenance:** Ongoing monthly retainers of €100-€400

**United States:**
- **Basic Projects:** 3-4 weeks (higher quality expectations)
- **Standard Projects:** 6-8 weeks (more stakeholder input and revisions)
- **Premium Projects:** 10-16 weeks (extensive testing and optimization)
- **Maintenance:** Monthly retainers of $300-$1,200
