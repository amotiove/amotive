About Us

For over two decades, The Golden Fork has been Portland, OR's premier destination for exceptional dining experiences. Our commitment to culinary excellence and warm hospitality has made us a beloved institution in the community.

Our chef-driven menu showcases the finest seasonal ingredients, expertly prepared using both traditional techniques and innovative approaches. Every dish tells a story of passion, creativity, and respect for the craft of cooking.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying an evening out, our dedicated team ensures every moment at The Golden Fork is memorable. We believe that great food brings people together, and we're honored to be part of your dining story.
