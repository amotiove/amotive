Klaff Communication Principles

Based on Oren Klaff's "Flip the Script" methodology - we never push, we create conditions where prospects convince themselves.

🔄 Questions, Not Statements

❌ "We're the best web development agency"
✅ "What's your biggest challenge with your current site?"

📊 Status Matching

❌ "We'd love to work with you!"
✅ "We work with 3 clients max per quarter."

⚡ Flash Roll Expertise

❌ Long list of technical capabilities
✅ "Built 50+ conversion-focused sites. Moving on..."

🎯 Plain Vanilla First

❌ Leading with cutting-edge AI features
✅ "Clean design that converts visitors to customers."

Brand Application

✓ Consistent Applications

  • • Always use approved logo files
  • • Maintain color relationships across touchpoints
  • • Apply voice principles to all copy
  • • Use proper typography hierarchy
  • • Respect clear space requirements
  • • Test on mobile devices

✗ Brand Violations

  • • Recreating logos from scratch
  • • Using unapproved color combinations
  • • Pushy or desperate messaging
  • • Corporate jargon or buzzwords
  • • Ignoring minimum size requirements
  • • Adding unauthorized effects or treatments

🎯 Brand Success Metrics

A successful brand application should:

  • Be immediately recognizable as Amotive
  • Convey premium quality without pretension
  • Make the prospect think, not just react
  • Position us as equals, not service providers
  • Focus on results, not features
