# Monthly Website Management Agreement

**ONGOING WEBSITE MAINTENANCE AND SUPPORT SERVICES**

This Monthly Management Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] ("Effective Date") between Amotive Design LLC, a Washington limited liability company ("Company" or "Amotive"), and [CLIENT LEGAL NAME] ("Client").

## 1. SERVICE TIERS AND PRICING

### TIER 1: BASIC MAINTENANCE - $100/month
**Included Services:**
- Monthly security updates
- Plugin and theme updates
- Weekly automated backups
- Monthly uptime monitoring report
- Basic malware scanning
- Emergency support (business hours only)
- Up to 1 hour of minor edits/changes per month

**Excluded Services:**
- Content creation or major updates
- Design changes
- New functionality
- SEO services
- Performance optimization beyond basic updates

### TIER 2: PROFESSIONAL MANAGEMENT - $150/month
**Included Services:**
- All Tier 1 services, plus:
- Daily security monitoring
- Performance optimization (monthly)
- Content updates (up to 3 per month)
- Image optimization and compression
- Broken link monitoring and repair
- Basic SEO health checks
- Priority support (4-hour response time)
- Up to 2 hours of edits/changes per month

**Excluded Services:**
- Major design overhauls
- Custom development
- Advanced SEO campaigns
- E-commerce management
- Social media management

### TIER 3: PREMIUM SUPPORT - $200/month
**Included Services:**
- All Tier 2 services, plus:
- 24/7 emergency support
- Advanced security monitoring and threat response
- Weekly performance reports
- Unlimited minor content updates
- Basic e-commerce support (if applicable)
- Monthly analytics reports
- Advanced backup management (multiple daily backups)
- Up to 4 hours of edits/changes per month
- One free consultation call per month (30 minutes)

**Excluded Services:**
- Complete website redesigns
- Custom application development
- Paid advertising management
- Full e-commerce setup/migration

## 2. PAYMENT TERMS

### 2.1 Billing Schedule
- **Monthly billing in advance**
- Payment due on the 1st of each month
- First month prorated based on start date
- Late payments incur 1.5% monthly service charge (18% annually)

### 2.2 Automatic Renewal
This Agreement automatically renews for successive one-month terms unless terminated by either party with proper notice.

### 2.3 Price Increase Clause
Company may increase prices with sixty (60) days written notice. Price increases shall not exceed 15% annually. Client may terminate without penalty if price increase exceeds 10% in any 12-month period.

### 2.4 Overage Charges
Work exceeding included hours will be billed at:
- Tier 1: $75/hour
- Tier 2: $85/hour  
- Tier 3: $95/hour

## 3. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 3.1 Initial Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues on a month-to-month basis.

### 3.2 Termination by Client
Client may terminate with thirty (30) days written notice to Company. Termination is effective at the end of the current billing period.

### 3.3 Termination by Company
Company may terminate with thirty (30) days written notice for any reason, or immediately for:
- Non-payment after 10-day cure period
- Material breach of Agreement terms
- Client's hostile or abusive conduct toward Company personnel

### 3.4 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Client remains liable for all charges through termination date
- Company will provide final backup of website files
- Support services cease immediately upon termination
- Client responsible for arranging alternative hosting/support

### 3.5 No Refunds
Monthly fees are non-refundable regardless of termination reason or timing.

## 4. SCOPE AND LIMITATIONS

### 4.1 Response Times
- Tier 1: Business hours support (48-hour response)
- Tier 2: Priority support (4-hour response during business hours)
- Tier 3: 24/7 emergency support (1-hour response for emergencies)

**Business Hours:** Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM Pacific Time, excluding holidays

### 4.2 Emergency Definition
Emergency situations include:
- Website completely offline
- Security breach or malware infection
- Database corruption affecting functionality
- Critical functionality failure

Non-emergencies include:
- Minor visual issues
- Content update requests
- Performance optimization
- Feature enhancement requests

### 4.3 Excluded Services
The following services require separate agreements:
- Complete website redesigns
- Custom development projects
- E-commerce setup or major modifications
- SEO campaigns and content marketing
- Paid advertising management
- Social media management
- Email marketing services
- Domain or hosting migration

## 5. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

### 5.1 Access Requirements
Client must provide and maintain:
- Administrative access to website
- Hosting account access
- Domain registrar access
- Any third-party service credentials needed for maintenance

### 5.2 Content and Materials
Client is responsible for:
- Providing content and materials for updates
- Ensuring content accuracy and legal compliance
- Backing up critical business data independently
- Maintaining current contact information

### 5.3 Third-Party Services
Client is responsible for:
- Payment of hosting, domain, and third-party service fees
- Maintaining third-party service accounts in good standing
- Communicating any changes to third-party services

## 6. LIMITATIONS AND DISCLAIMERS

### 6.1 Hosting and Third-Party Dependencies
Company's ability to provide services depends on:
- Hosting provider performance and reliability
- Domain and DNS service availability
- Third-party plugin and theme updates
- Internet connectivity and infrastructure

Company is not liable for service interruptions caused by third-party providers.

### 6.2 Backup Limitations
While Company provides regular backups as part of service tiers, Client is encouraged to maintain independent backups. Company does not guarantee backup completeness or restoration success in all circumstances.

### 6.3 Security Disclaimer
Company employs industry-standard security practices but cannot guarantee absolute security. Client remains responsible for strong password policies and secure account management.

## 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 7.1 Existing IP
Client retains ownership of existing website content and branding. Company retains ownership of proprietary tools, methodologies, and code.

### 7.2 Work Product
Minor modifications and updates performed under this Agreement become part of the existing website without separate IP ownership issues.

## 8. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### 8.1 Liability Cap
Company's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed three (3) months of Client's monthly service fee.

### 8.2 Force Majeure
Company shall not be liable for service delays or failures caused by circumstances beyond reasonable control.

### 8.3 Client Indemnification
Client agrees to indemnify Company against claims arising from Client's website content or use of services.

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information disclosed during the service relationship.

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 10.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington.

### 10.2 Dispute Resolution
Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Kitsap County, Washington.

### 10.3 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between parties for ongoing maintenance services.

### 10.4 Amendment
This Agreement may only be amended by written document signed by both parties.

### 10.5 Service Level Changes
Client may upgrade or downgrade service tiers with thirty (30) days written notice. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
