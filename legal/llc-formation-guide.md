# Washington State LLC Formation Guide

## Age Requirements & Options

**CRITICAL:** Must be 18+ to file LLC in Washington State

### Option A: Wait Until 18th Birthday
- File LLC the day you turn 18
- Complete ownership and control immediately
- **Recommended approach** for clean ownership

### Option B: Parent/Guardian Formation (Current Option)
- Parent/guardian files LLC now
- Operating Agreement specifies transfer at 18
- More complex, requires attorney review
- Potential gift tax considerations

## Step-by-Step Filing Process

### 1. Choose LLC Name
**Requirements:**
- Must contain "Limited Liability Company," "LLC," or "L.L.C."
- Must be distinguishable from existing WA entities
- Cannot contain restricted words without approval

**Check availability:** https://www.sos.wa.gov/corps/search.aspx

**Suggested Names:**
- Amotive Design LLC
- Amotive Web Solutions LLC
- Amotive Digital LLC

### 2. Registered Agent
**Requirements:**
- Must be 18+ and WA State resident, OR
- Business entity authorized to do business in WA
- Must have WA physical address (not P.O. Box)

**Options:**
- Self (once 18): Free, but public record
- Professional service: $100-300/year, privacy protection
- Parent/guardian (temporary): If using Option B above

**Recommended Services:**
- Northwest Registered Agent: $125/year
- Incfile: $119/year
- LegalZoom: $159/year

### 3. File Certificate of Formation

**Where:** Washington State Secretary of State
**Website:** https://www.sos.wa.gov/corps/
**Filing Fee:** $200 (non-refundable)
**Processing:** 1-2 business days online, 7-10 days by mail

**Required Information:**
- LLC name
- Registered agent name and address
- Principal office address
- Duration (perpetual recommended)
- Management structure (member-managed recommended)
- Organizer information

**Online Filing Steps:**
1. Create SecState account
2. Select "Form an LLC"
3. Complete Certificate of Formation
4. Pay $200 fee
5. Receive confirmation and file number

### 4. Operating Agreement

**CRITICAL:** Even for single-member LLC
**Benefits:**
- Protects limited liability status
- Defines management structure
- Banking and tax elections
- Succession planning

## Basic Operating Agreement Template

```
OPERATING AGREEMENT OF AMOTIVE DESIGN LLC

Article I - Organization
1.1 Formation: This Limited Liability Company was formed under Washington Revised Code Chapter 25.15.

1.2 Name: The name of the Company is "Amotive Design LLC"

1.3 Principal Office: [Address]

1.4 Registered Agent: [Name and Address]

Article II - Members and Ownership
2.1 Initial Member: [Name], owning 100% membership interest

2.2 Capital Contribution: $1,000 initial contribution

Article III - Management
3.1 Management Structure: Member-managed LLC

3.2 Authority: Member has full authority to bind the Company

Article IV - Financial Provisions
4.1 Capital Accounts: Maintained per Member contributions

4.2 Distributions: At Member's discretion

4.3 Banking: Member authorized to open accounts and conduct banking

Article V - Tax Elections
5.1 Tax Status: Elect disregarded entity status for federal taxes

Article VI - Dissolution
6.1 Voluntary Dissolution: Upon Member's written consent

[ATTORNEY REVIEW RECOMMENDED]
```

### 5. Post-Formation Requirements

#### EIN (Federal Tax ID)
- Apply immediately after LLC formation
- See separate EIN guide
- Required for banking and taxes

#### Business Licenses
- Check city, county, and state requirements
- See separate business license guide
- Professional service licenses may apply

#### Bank Account
- Open business account within 30 days
- Requires: Certificate of Formation, EIN, Operating Agreement
- Keep business and personal finances separate

#### Washington State Business License
- File within 30 days of starting business
- Basic license: $19/year
- File at: https://bls.dor.wa.gov/

### 6. Annual Requirements

#### Annual Report
- Due: End of birth month of LLC
- Fee: $71
- File online at Secretary of State website
- Failure results in $10/month penalty + dissolution risk

#### Tax Returns
- WA B&O Tax: Monthly/quarterly depending on revenue
- Federal: Form 1040 Schedule C (single-member)
- See separate tax guide

## Important Deadlines

1. **File LLC** - When eligible (age 18)
2. **Apply for EIN** - Within 30 days
3. **Business licenses** - Within 30 days of starting operations
4. **First annual report** - End of birth month following formation year
