# Legal Compliance

## INVOICE REQUIREMENTS

**Washington State B&O Tax:**
- Include business license number if required
- Track gross receipts for all invoices
- Report monthly/quarterly/annually based on revenue

**Federal Requirements:**
- Include EIN on business invoices
- Issue 1099s to contractors paid >$600 annually
- Maintain records for 7 years

## COLLECTION PROCEDURES

**Internal Collections:**
- Follow systematic reminder schedule
- Document all collection efforts
- Maintain professional communication

**External Collections:**
- Consider collection agency after 60-90 days
- Evaluate cost vs. recovery amount
- Consult attorney for large unpaid amounts

**Legal Action:**
- Small claims court for amounts under $5,000
- Superior court for larger amounts
- Consider arbitration clauses in contracts
