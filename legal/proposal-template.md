## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What if I need changes after the website is complete?**
A: Minor updates are covered under our 30-day warranty. Larger changes or new features can be handled through our maintenance service or separate project agreements.

**Q: Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?**
A: Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages starting at $100/month. This includes security updates, backups, content updates, and technical support.

**Q: What happens if I'm not satisfied with the design?**
A: We include two rounds of revisions to ensure you're happy with the design. Our goal is 100% client satisfaction, and we'll work with you to achieve the results you want.

**Q: How do I update content after launch?**
A: We'll provide training on your content management system, allowing you to make updates yourself. We're also available for content updates as part of our maintenance service.

**Q: What if my timeline needs to change?**
A: We understand business priorities can shift. Contact us as soon as possible to discuss timeline adjustments. Some changes may affect the project cost or launch date.

**Q: Do you offer hosting and domain services?**
A: We can set up hosting and domain registration as part of your project. These services are billed separately on an annual basis.
