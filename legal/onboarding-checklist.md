# Client Onboarding Checklist

**AMOTIVE DESIGN LLC - NEW CLIENT ONBOARDING PROCESS**

---

## PRE-PROJECT REQUIREMENTS

### 1. LEGAL DOCUMENTATION ✅

**Must Have Before Starting Work:**

**Master Service Agreement**
- [ ] Client has reviewed and signed MSA
- [ ] All terms and conditions agreed upon
- [ ] Electronic or physical signature received
- [ ] Signed copy filed in client folder

**Project Scope Document**
- [ ] Detailed scope of work documented
- [ ] Timeline and milestones agreed upon
- [ ] Deliverables clearly defined
- [ ] Change order process explained

**Insurance Verification**
- [ ] E&O insurance current and adequate
- [ ] Client added as additional insured (if required)
- [ ] Coverage limits meet project requirements

### 2. FINANCIAL SETUP ✅

**Payment Processing**
- [ ] 50% retainer payment received ($750 minimum)
- [ ] Payment method verified and working
- [ ] Invoice system set up for client
- [ ] Final payment schedule confirmed

**Financial Information**
- [ ] Client billing address confirmed
- [ ] Payment terms understood (Net 30 days)
- [ ] Late fee policy acknowledged
- [ ] Tax information gathered (for 1099 if applicable)

### 3. BUSINESS INFORMATION ✅

**Company Details**
- [ ] Legal business name verified
- [ ] DBA/trade names identified
- [ ] Business address (physical and mailing)
- [ ] Primary contact person designated
- [ ] Backup contact person identified

**Contact Information**
- [ ] Primary email addresses
- [ ] Phone numbers (office, mobile, fax)
- [ ] Best times to reach client
- [ ] Communication preferences (email, phone, text)
- [ ] Time zone considerations

### RISK MITIGATION CHECKLIST

**Red Flags to Watch For:**
- [ ] Incomplete or poor quality content
- [ ] Unrealistic timeline expectations
- [ ] Unclear decision-making authority
- [ ] Payment delays or issues
- [ ] Scope creep attempts

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- [ ] Clear documentation of all requirements
- [ ] Firm boundaries on scope and timeline
- [ ] Regular communication and updates
- [ ] Quick response to issues or concerns
- [ ] Professional but firm contract enforcement
