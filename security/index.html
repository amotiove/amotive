What's Included

Four layers of protection.

Bot Protection

Advanced threat filtering stops malicious bots, scrapers, and automated attacks before they reach your site. Our multi-layered defense includes DDoS mitigation, rate limiting, and intelligent pattern detection that adapts to evolving threats — keeping your site safe without slowing it down for real visitors.

SSL Encryption

HTTPS everywhere with TLS 1.3 — the latest encryption standard. Every connection between your visitors and your site is encrypted end-to-end. Certificates renew automatically so your protection never lapses. That padlock icon in the browser bar isn't optional — it's standard.

99.9% Uptime

Your site stays online because your business depends on it. Redundant infrastructure across multiple regions, global CDN distribution, and instant failover mean your site is always available — even during traffic spikes or infrastructure issues. Downtime isn't a risk we accept.

Daily Backups

Automated daily snapshots of your entire site — code, content, databases, everything. Stored securely offsite with point-in-time recovery so we can restore your site to any moment in the last 30 days. Accidents happen. With daily backups, they don't become disasters.
