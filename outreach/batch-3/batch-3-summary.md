## Campaign Strategy

### Klaff Inception Method Applied:
- **Never Push:** Each email ends with soft questions, not demands
- **Create Curiosity:** Lead with interesting observations about their business
- **Flash Expertise:** Drop specific stats and insights, then stop
- **No Corporate Garbage:** Human voice, conversational tone
- **Specific References:** Every email mentions something unique about their business

### Pain Point Categories:
1. **Invisible Online:** Missing customers who can't find them
2. **Lost Credibility:** Broken/outdated sites suggest unprofessional business  
3. **Mobile Problems:** Tourism market can't access on phones
4. **Wasted Marketing:** Paying for ads with no follow-through

### Offer Positioning:
- **Fixed Price:** $1,500 (no surprises)
- **Fast Delivery:** 5 business days (urgency)
- **No Ongoing Fees:** One-time investment (removes barrier)
