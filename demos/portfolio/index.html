About Me

With over a decade of experience behind the lens, I specialize in capturing the authentic beauty in every moment. My work spans from intimate portraits to grand architectural photography, always seeking the story within each frame.

Based in Barcelona, I draw inspiration from the city's vibrant culture and stunning architecture. My approach combines technical precision with artistic vision, creating images that not only document but truly resonate with the viewer's emotions.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring hidden corners of the city, always on the lookout for that perfect interplay of light and shadow that defines my signature style.
